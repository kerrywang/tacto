About Tacto

Tacto’s goal is to bring the sense of touch to virtual and augmented reality. We offer solutions to the unmet needs facing AR/VR users:

  • Enhanced immersion
    • Current video and audio in VR are great. But when you reach out, you can’t feel anything but air, or you’re already holding a controller. Tacto lets you touch the virtual world.

  • Intuitive control
    • No buttons or rigid controllers necessary. Tacto’s glove will automatically know where your hands are so you don’t have to think twice about picking something up.

  • Natural movement & Interaction
    • With combined feedback and control, Tacto redefines virtual interaction, allowing you to use your hands like you would in the real world. No more moving like a robot.

    VR Gloves

    Haptic glove using vibrational feedback with proven actuator positions to let you reach out and feel the virtual world

    SDK

    Library of haptic driving patterns developed by our state of the art testing application to accurately provide haptic feedbacks
